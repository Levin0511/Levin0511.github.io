Warung Pok Nong

Warung Pok Nong is one of the most popular places to eat in Kuala Terengganu. Your experience in Kuala Terengganu will not be complete without trying the food at Warung Pok Nong. With reasonable prices, Warung Pok Nong offers a variety of seafood dishes that are sure to be delicious.
Rusila Pancake Restaurant

If your stomach is hungry but you don't want to eat rice, come to Restaurant Lempeng Rusila. This restaurant sells very tasty pancakes and can make you addicted.

D'Ganu Café Steamboat

In the middle of the cold night wind, it is the most delicious to eat hot food. If you are looking for a restaurant with a romantic atmosphere that is suitable for dinner, come to D'Ganu Café Steamboat. Here, you can enjoy a delicious meal while enjoying the view of the river and the soothing sea breeze.
Mat Binjai Restaurant

It would be a loss if you come to Kuala Terengganu without trying village cuisine and Terengganu State cuisine. Please visit Mat Binjai Restaurant to enjoy the special dishes of Kuala Terengganu.

Warung Pok Nong


One of the famous dishes here is Flour Dip Fish. The fresh fish coated in crispy flour makes this dish a crowd favorite. You can also try other dishes such as flour-dipped shrimp and flour-dipped squid. Warung Pok Nong also serves a variety of fresh drinks such as corn ice, peanut ice, and coconut ice. Don't miss the chance to taste the delicious seafood at this restaurant! Let's visit immediately!

Address: Jalan Pantai, A1693, T145, Kampung Teliput, 21030 Kuala Terengganu

Rusila Pancake Restaurant


This pancake made of flour, coconut milk, and young coconut filling will be even more delicious if eaten with anchovy sauce. Not only pancakes, this restaurant also offers a variety of other foods that you must try such as ketupat sotong, rojok kateh, nasi dagang, and so on. Need something fresh? Don't worry. The restaurant also sells fresh coconut water and ice cream. Overall, complete!

Address: 11, Jalan Mawar 3, 21080 Kuala Terengganu

D'Ganu Café Steamboat


This restaurant serves several types of food including seafood, western food, and local food that is sure to tempt the palate. The food options are also diverse, including chicken, meat, squid, shrimp, and vegetables. The most famous in this restaurant, as the name suggests, is of course the steamboat. In fact, the steamboat served at this restaurant is considered to be the best in Kuala Terengganu. Enjoy the experience of enjoying a steamboat with delicious tom yam sauce and sauce, accompanied by the sound of refreshing sea waves.

Address: Pulau Duyung Kecil, 20100 Kuala Terengganu

Mat Binjai Restaurant


This eatery serves village-style dishes such as white rice with a variety of side dishes such as sambal catfish, lemak fish stew, grilled squid, mixed lemak vegetables, and fried kampung chicken. Complements such as sambal belacan, budu, and side dishes are also provided. What's interesting, all dishes are served to customers by serving, making Mat Binjai Restaurant suitable for group visits with friends or family.

Address: 1081-Q, Jalan Sultan Sulaiman, 20000 Kuala Terengganu
