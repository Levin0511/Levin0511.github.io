Blackwood Coffee & Chocolate, Arau


Their industrial-chic interior of exposed brick walls and pipes gives the cafe its trendy ambience. Serving mainly Western dishes, this cafe is the perfect spot for good coffee, and we also recommend trying their ample variety of crepe cakes!

Address: Lot 3968 Tingkat Bawah, Kompleks Arau, 02600 Arau, Perlis

Hai Thien, Kuala Perlis


Famed for its steamed fish dishes, cakoi (a long piece of fried dough), and fried ice cream, this restaurant is a must-try when in town. We’re sure we had you at fried ice cream!

Address: Jalan Jeti Baru, 02000 Kuala Perlis, Perlis

Warung Pokok Sawa, Kangar


Freshly done from the stove by 11am, you’ll find that they often get sold out by 2pm, so get there on time! RM20 will get you by with about three to four regular-portioned dishes. For inquiries, you can quickly get in touch through WhatsApp!

Address: Kampung Kersik, Mata Ayer, 02500 Kangar, Perlis

Citarasa Mona Ikan Bakar Komalaut, Kuala Perlis


You can never get enough seafood, and in Kuala Perlis, there are plenty of seafood restaurants to choose from. Another one worth mentioning is Citarasa Mona Ikan Bakar Komalaut, where they will personally recommend dishes that will have you coming back for seconds! Give their chilli cream and fried squid a go too! RM200 will easily feed six to seven pax with an order of about five dishes.

Address: Pekan Kuala Perlis, 02000 Kuala Perlis, Perlis
