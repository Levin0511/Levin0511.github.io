Authentic Qiaotou Fujian Noodles


You need to be patient to eat noodles here. The noodles are fried in plates, and then stir-fried with lard and dark soy sauce for a while, and you will smell a fragrant aroma. People in Alor Setar who love spicy food will definitely add green chili, which is salty and spicy, making people unable to stop!

Address: Jalan Pintu Sepuluh, Kampung Lubok Peringgi, 05100 Alor Setar, Kedah

Address: 133, Jalan Langgar, Alor Setar 05460 Kedah
