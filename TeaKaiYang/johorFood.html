Kluang Kopitiam


Kluang Kopitiam is a traditional coffee shop located in Kluang, Johor, Malaysia. Established in 1938, it is renowned for its rich coffee, handmade bread, and authentic Malaysian-style breakfast. The shop maintains a classic decor and nostalgic atmosphere, making it a favorite among locals and visitors alike. Kluang Kopitiam offers traditional Kaya Toast, half-boiled eggs, coffee, and various Malaysian snacks, providing a warm and nostalgic dining experience.


Address: KTM Jalan Station, 86000 Kluang

K&Y Otak-Otak


K&Y Otak-Otak is a popular eatery known for its specialty in "otak-otak," a traditional Southeast Asian fish cake. The dish is made from fish paste mixed with spices and herbs, wrapped in banana leaves, and grilled to perfection. This eatery offers a range of otak-otak varieties, often complemented by a selection of local side dishes and condiments.

Address: Jalan Bentayan, Muar, Malaysia

B.P. Cendol


B.P. Cendol is a well-known establishment specializing in "cendol," a traditional Southeast Asian dessert. Cendol is a refreshing treat made with a mixture of shaved ice, coconut milk, palm sugar syrup, and green jelly-like strands made from rice flour. It's often garnished with red beans, corn, or other toppings.

Address: 65-A, Jalan Rugayah, Kampung Pegawai, 83000 Batu Pahat, Johor

Yong’s Steamboat


Yong's Steamboat is a popular dining spot known for its steamboat (hot pot) dishes. In a steamboat meal, diners cook a variety of ingredients such as thinly sliced meats, seafood, vegetables, and noodles in a pot of simmering broth right at the table. Yong's Steamboat is recognized for its high-quality ingredients, flavorful broths, and a wide selection of fresh items for cooking.

Address:No. 2 M/S, Jalan Minyak Beku, Batu Pahat, Johor, Malaysia.

Ah Fei Spicy Noodle


Ah Fei Spicy Noodle is a popular restaurant known for its spicy noodle dishes. The establishment specializes in offering a range of spicy noodle options, often featuring a mix of bold flavors and heat. Their menu typically includes various types of noodles paired with spicy sauces, meats, vegetables, and sometimes seafood.

Address: 2, Jalan Jenang, Kampung Pegawai, 83000 Batu Pahat, Johor

Restoran Woon Kiang


The restaurant is known for its welcoming atmosphere and excellent service, making it a favorite choice for both locals and visitors. Dishes might include a variety of stir-fries, noodles, soups, and other classic dishes, all prepared with fresh ingredients and traditional cooking techniques. Restoran Woon Kiang is a great place to experience authentic flavors and enjoy a satisfying meal in a comfortable setting.

Address: Jalan Pandan 1, Taman Stulang, 80300 Johor Bahru, Johor

Hong Kong Boy Johor Jaya Food Street


Hong Kong Boy Johor Jaya Food Street is a vibrant eatery located in Johor Jaya, Johor, Malaysia. This restaurant is known for its variety of Hong Kong-style dishes and street food, offering a taste of authentic Hong Kong cuisine. The menu typically includes popular items such as dim sum, roasted meats, congee, and noodles, all prepared with traditional flavors and techniques.

Address: 2, Jalan Dedap 22, Johor Jaya, 81100 Johor Bahru

Poh Kee Restaurant


Poh Kee Restaurant is a well-known dining establishment that offers a range of traditional Malaysian and Chinese dishes. Located in Malaysia, the restaurant is praised for its flavorful and authentic cuisine, which often includes dishes like roasted meats, stir-fried vegetables, and various rice and noodle dishes.

Address: Taman Desa Tebrau (52 Jalan Harmonium 23/13)81100 Johor Bahru, Johor
