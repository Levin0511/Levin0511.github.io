Restoran New Tea House


Jinhua Cake Teahouse is a famous traditional teahouse in Rau. In addition to the most basic traditional tea such as coffee, milk tea and steamed bread, its spiced siu Bao, Jiayang Jiao, barbecued chicken shredded Jiao, curry chicken Jiao, etc., are many people's favorites. Their famous Jiayang jiao wins the cake crust is thin and crispy, and the taste is delicious. But the owner, Zhang Guoshun, said that the best thing his family sold was actually wedding cakes!

Address: 11, Bukit Koman, 27600, Raub, Pahang.

Ah Ming Char Kuey Teow


Right next to Ah Fa Seafood Restaurant, the short son fried kway teow is a local must-have snack that has been sold for more than 30 years. At 7 p.m., a file opened on the source of customers, sold until 11 p.m., the file was closed, and the business was hot to no good. If you want a taste, it's best to call in advance and order, or you'll have to wait in front of the stall. Its fried kway teow is characterized by more juice, salty taste with sweet, and thin and smooth kway teow.


Address: 12, Lorong Tun Razak, 27600, Raub, Pahang.

Ah Fa Seafood Restaurant


In Rau, Yafa Seafood Restaurant is well known and is the first choice for local people to hold wedding banquets. Even "Hong Kong food God" Leung Man-tao group to Ma to find food, but also specifically designated to eat in Asia, which shows that its delicious recognition. In addition to the traditional Hakka dishes such as taro pork and Hakka salted chicken can be eaten here, the exquisite handmade dishes such as cold chicken and chessboard duck are rare dishes. But owner Cao Ruixing said the handmade dishes are not available every day because of the complexity of the process and must be booked in advance.


Address: 82, Jalan Razak, 27600, Raub, Pahang.

Restoran Lin Chi Kang


If you are in Kuantan, try the flour cake at Restoran Lin Chi Kang Taman Kuantan! Yes, it's one of those mouthwatering noodles! Don't be put off by its bright colors, this tricolor panel is not dyed with artificial colors! Red is dragon fruit, yellow is radish, cyan is spinach, all made of natural ingredients. And the taste is definitely called the teeth, so that you can not stop one mouthful. What's more, this three-color flour cake is also rich in the nutritional fiber of various vegetables, which is delicious and healthy, and guaranteed to leave you with a long taste!


Address: A4392, Jln Teluk Sisek, Taman Plaza, 25050 Kuantan, Pahang

Alor Akar Seafood Restaurant


When you come to Kuantan, don't miss the famous Beggar Chicken Alor Akar Seafood Restaurant! This restaurant is famous for its signature dish, Beggar's chicken. When you open the whole chicken wrapped in foil, a strong aroma comes to your face, because this beggar chicken is braised with more than a dozen precious herbs, which are full of taste and make people mouth watering! The chicken is soft and tender, and the bones separate easily.


Address: Lot 7695 & 7696, Jalan Kubang Buaya, Taman Mariana, 25300 Kuantan, Pahang

Restoran Hoo Seng


At Restoran Hoo Seng, one of Kuantan's favourite dishes is Dongguan braised Duck Noodles! The noodles here look like wonton noodles, but coarser and chewy, and each bite is full of elasticity and tenacity that you can't put down. But the most attractive is the rich roast duck marinade and tender roast duck meat, the unique seasoning makes them a perfect match.


Address: a6308, Jalan Beserah, Kampung Galing Besar, 25300 Kuantan, Pahang
